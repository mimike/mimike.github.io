About

Aerialist turned Software Developer

I am interested in making beautiful, user efficient web applications.  My background in the arts and movement practices informs my approach to web design and coding.  After over a decade teaching aerial and yoga, I have honed my ability to break down complex movement patterns and dissect large problems into small pieces. I approach my code in the same way and find many parallels between life on the mat, in the air and creating programs.
